# 🟡 Medium Recursion Problems

These problems introduce backtracking and recursion with combinations.

| # | Problem | Concept |
|---|---------|---------|
| 1 | Power(x, n) | Divide & Conquer |
| 2 | Generate Parentheses | Backtracking |
| 3 | Letter Combinations of a Phone Number | Recursion + Backtracking |
| 4 | Permutations | Backtracking |
| 5 | Subsets | Pick / Not Pick |
| 6 | Combination Sum | Backtracking |
| 7 | Combination Sum II | Backtracking + Duplicate Handling |
