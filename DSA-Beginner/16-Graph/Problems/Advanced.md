# 🔴 Advanced Graph Problems

These are advanced graph challenges commonly asked in product company interviews.

| # | Problem | Concept |
|---|---------|---------|
| 1 | Word Ladder | BFS |
| 2 | Alien Dictionary | Topological Sort |
| 3 | Cheapest Flights Within K Stops | BFS + Dijkstra |
| 4 | Critical Connections in a Network | Tarjan's Algorithm |
| 5 | Redundant Connection | Union Find (DSU) |
| 6 | Minimum Cost to Connect All Points | Minimum Spanning Tree (Kruskal/Prim) |
| 7 | Swim in Rising Water | Dijkstra + Priority Queue |
