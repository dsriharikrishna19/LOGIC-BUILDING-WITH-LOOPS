| 1 | Single Number III | XOR Partition |

| 5 | Minimize XOR | Greedy + Bits |
| 6 | Find XOR Sum of All Pairs Bitwise AND | Bitwise Math |
| 7 | Number of Excellent Pairs | Bit Counting + HashSet |
